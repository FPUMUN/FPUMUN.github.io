Who are we?

Our mission

To provide a space for young diplomats to discuss social conflicts occurring in our world nowadays through negotiating and debating.

Our vision

To construct an influential role for youth in an everyday world.

Our values

  • Respect
  • Diversity
  • Unity
  • Peace
  • Development
